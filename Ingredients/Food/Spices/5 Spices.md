*Five-spice powder* is a mixture of five or more spices in Chinese cuisine. Commonly, it consists of [[Star Anise|star anise]], [[Cloves|cloves]], [[Chinese Cinnamon|Chinese cinnamon]], [[Sichuan Pepper|sichuan pepper]], and [[Fennel Seed|fennel seed]].
The mixture is often used as a spice rub for [[Chicken|chicken]], [[Duck|duck]], [[Goose|goose]], [[Pork|pork]], and seafood, but it is also often added to the breading of fried food. Along with this, it is used in grilling, saut√©ing, #RecipeBook/TypeOfFood/Stirfry, rice, and noodle dishes.

Traditionally, the mixture is also used as an antiseptic and a cure for indigestion - primarily through high antioxidant contents of cloves.
