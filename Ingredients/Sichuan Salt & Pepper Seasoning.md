# Ingredients
- 1 tsp [[Sichuan Pepper|sichuan pepper]]
- ½ [[Star Anise|star anise]]
- ½ tsp [[Sesame Seed|sesame seed]]
- ¼ tsp [[Fennel Seed|fennel seed]]
- 4 tsp [[Salt|salt]]
# Notes
- ± 6 tsp yield
- Requirements: [[Mortar & Pestle|mortar & pestle]]
- Time: 10 minutes
# Directions
1. Put the sichuan pepper, star anise, sesame seeds, fennel seeds and salt into a pan. Toast over low heat while stirring from time to time.
2. Once the salt darkens slightly and the spices become very fragrant, remove from the heat and then grind in a mortar or spice grinder.
