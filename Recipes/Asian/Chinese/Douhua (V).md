# Ingredients
- 325 ml [[Soy Milk|soy milk]]
- 1/2 tsp [[Agar|agar]]
# Notes
- X servings
- Requirements:
- Time: 
# Directions
1. Combine the soy milk and agar in a small pot, and stir to incorporate.
2. Heat over medium-high heat, stirring constantly, until bringing to a light boil. Turn to medium-low heat to reduce it to a low simmer, then cook for another 5 minutes or until the agar is fully dissolved.
3. Pour the mixture into a heat proof bowl. Cover with a few layers of paper towels and secure with a lid or large plate. Transfer the bowl to the refrigerator and let it rest for at least 1 hour.
