# Ingredients
- 400g [[Flour|flour]]
- 200g [[Brown Sugar|brown sugar]]
- 350ml [[Milk|milk]]
- 80ml [[Rum|rum]]
- 100ml [[Oil|oil]]
- 12g [[Baking Powder|baking powder]]
- 2 tbsp [[Cocoa Powder|cocoa powder]]
- 1/2 tsp [[Vanilla|vanilla]]
# Notes
- 16 servings
- Requirements: mixer, 2 mixing bowls
- Time: 
# Directions
1. Sift the flour, and add the sugar, a pinch of [[Salt|salt]], the baking powder, and the vanilla.
2. In the second bowl, mix the milk, rum, and oil. Add to the flour.
3. Mix everything together until there are no traces of flour left.
4. Divide the batter into two equal parts. Mix the cocoa into one part.  Add a tablespoon of milk to make sure it doesn't become too thick.
5. Grease a cake tin with [[Butter|Butter]] and flour it lightly. Add the batter to own insight.
6. Bake the cake for 40-45 minutes at 180C.
7. Check the cake with a toothpick. If it comes out dry, the cake is done. Let cool before taking it out of the tin. Garnish with [[Powdered Sugar|powdered sugar]] and nuts.
