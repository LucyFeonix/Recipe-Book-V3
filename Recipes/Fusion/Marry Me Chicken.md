# Ingredients
- 3 tbsp [[Olive Oil|extra virgin olive oil]]
- 4 [[Chicken Breast|chicken breasts]]
- 2 cloves [[Garlic|garlic]], finely chopped
- 1 tbsp [[Thyme|fresh thyme]]
- 1 tsp [[Crushed Red Pepper Flakes|crushed red pepper flakes]]
- ¾ cup [[Chicken Broth|chicken broth]]
- ½ cup [[Sun-dried Tomatoes|sun-dried tomatoes]], finely chopped
- ½ cup [[Heavy Cream|heavy cream]]
- ¼ cup [[Parmesan Cheese|parmesan cheese]], finely grated
- *[[Sweet Basil|Basil]], for garnish*
# Notes
- 4 servings
- Requirements:
- Time: 40 minutes
# Directions
1. Arrange a rack in the centre of an oven, preheat to 375°F. In a large ovenproof skillet over medium-high heat, heat a tablespoon of oil. Generously season the chicken with [[Salt|salt]] and [[Black Pepper|pepper]], and cook until golden brown, turning halfway through. Transfer the chicken to a plate.
2. In the same skillet over medium heat, heat the remaining oil. Stir in the garlic, thyme, and red pepper flakes. Cook, stirring, until fragrant (± 1 minute). Stir in the broth, tomatoes, cream, and parmesan, and add salt to taste. Bring to a simmer, and return the chicken and any accumulated juices to the skillet.
3. Transfer the skillet to the oven. Bake chicken until cooked through, 10 to 12 minutes.
4. Arrange the chicken on a platter, spoon sauce over. *Garnish with basil.*
