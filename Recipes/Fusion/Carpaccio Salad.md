# Ingredients
- 120g [[Carpaccio|carpaccio]], shredded
- 150g [[Romaine Lettuce|romaine lettuce]], shredded
- 120g [[Sun-dried Tomatoes|sun-dried tomatoes]], diced
- 2 [[Egg|eggs]]
- 50g [[Croutons|croutons]]
- 2 tsp [[Truffle Mayonnaise|truffle mayonnaise]]
- Dash of [[Lemon Juice|lemon juice]]
- 1 tbsp [[Cashew|cashews]]
