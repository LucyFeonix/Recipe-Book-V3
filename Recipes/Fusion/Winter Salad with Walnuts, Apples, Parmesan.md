# Ingredients
- 1/2 cup [[Mayonnaise]]
- 1 oz [[Cheese|parmesan cheese]]
- 6 [[Anchovy|anchovy fillets]], mashed into a paste with the back of a fork
- 2 tsp [[Lemon|lemon juice]]
- 1 tsp [[Worcestershire Sauce|worcestershire sauce]]
- 2 whole [[Endives|endives]], core removed, leaves cut into 1/8" slivers
- 1 head [[Radicchio|radicchio]], finely sliced
- 1 quart loosely packed [[Frisée|frisée]] fronds
- 1 large [[Fuji Apples|fuji apple]] or [[Granny Smith|granny smith]] apple, cut into matchsticks
- 1/4 cup [[Parsley|fresh parsley leaves, chopped]]
- 2 cups [[Walnuts|walnuts, toasted]]
# Notes
- 4 servings
- Requirements:
- Time: 20 minutes

# Directions
1. Combine the mayonnaise, parmesan, mashed anchovy, lemon juice, and Worcestershire sauce in a bowl, and whisk to combine. Season to taste with [[Salt|salt]] and [[Black Pepper|pepper]].
2. Toss the endive, radicchio, frisée, apples, and parsley, with the dressing. Add the nuts, and toss briefly. Serve immediately.
