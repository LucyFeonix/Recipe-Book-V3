# Ingredients
- 1 shot [[Blue Curaçao|blue curaçao]]
- [[Sprite|Sprite]]
- 1 tsp [[Sugar|sugar]]
# Notes
- 1 serving
- 1 [[Longdrink glass|longdrink glass]]
- Consists of 1.93% ABV
# Directions
1. Add the blue curaçao.
2. Top up with sprite.
3. Add the sugar.
