# Ingredients
- 200g [[Pasta|pasta]]
- 1 [[Chilli Pepper|chilli pepper]]
- 2 cloves [[Garlic|garlic]]
- Handful of [[Parsley|parsley]]
- 75mL [[Olive Oil|extra virgin olive oil]]
# Notes
- 2 servings
- Requirements:
- Time: 
# Directions
1. Cook the pasta al dente according to package instructions.
2. Mince the pepper and garlic, and roughly chop the parsley.
3. In a skillet, heat the oil over medium-high heat. Add the pepper and garlic, and fry for 1-2 minutes.
4. Drain the pasta, and return to the pan. Pour in the garlic-pepper-laden oil, and add the parsley and salt. Briefly warm up, and serve in deep plates.
