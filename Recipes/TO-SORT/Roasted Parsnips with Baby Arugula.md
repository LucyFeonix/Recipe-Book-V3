# Ingredients
- 1 lb [[Parsnip|parsnips]], trimmed, peeled and cut into ½ inch pieces
- 2 tbsp [[Olive Oil|extra virgin olive oil]]
- 2 tsp [[Maple Syrup|maple syrup]]
- 1 ½ cup [[Baby Arugula|baby arugula]]
# Notes
- 4 servings
- Requirements:
- Time: 
# Directions
1. Place a rack in the lowest position in the oven and preheat the oven to 375°F/190°C. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. Inn a large bowl, combine the parsnips, olive oil, maple syrup, and a large pinch of [[Salt|salt]] and [[Black Pepper|pepper]]. Spread in an even layer on the baking sheet. Roast the parsnips until they are tender and browned in spots, 35-40 minutes, stirring occasionally.
3. In the bowl used for the parsnips, gently toss the arugula with any residual oil and seasoning. Stir the arugula into the parsnips on the baking sheet and stir gently until some of the arugula starts to wilt.
4. Transfer the parsnips and arugula to a medium bowl and serve. An leftovers can be stored in the refrigerator up to 5 days.
