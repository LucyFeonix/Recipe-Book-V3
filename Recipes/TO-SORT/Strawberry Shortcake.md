# Ingredients
## Strawberries & Cream
- 6-7 cups [[Strawberry|strawberries]], quartered
- 75g [[Granulated Sugar|granulated sugar]], divided
- 1 tsp [[Vanilla Extract|vanilla extract]]
- 240mL [[Heavy Cream|heavy cream]]
## Biscuits
- 345g (2¾ cups) [[Flour|all-purpose flour]]
- 50g (¼ cup) [[Granulated Sugar|granulated sugar]]
- 4 tsp [[Baking Powder|baking powder]]
- ½ tsp [[Baking Soda|baking soda]]
- 1 tsp [[Salt|salt]]
- 170g (¾ cup) [[Unsalted Butter|unsalted butter]], cold and cubed
- 240mL (1 cup) cold [[Buttermilk|buttermilk]]
- 2 tbsp [[Heavy Cream|heavy cream]]
- *[[Sugar|Sugar]], coarse, for sprinkling.*

- 10-12 servings

# Directions
## Strawberries
1. Stir the strawberries and 50g of the granulated sugar together in a large bowl. Cover and set in the refrigerator until ready to use.
## Biscuits
1. Preheat the oven to 204°C/400°F.
2. Mix the flour, granulated sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a large bowl or in a food processor. Whisk or pulse until combined.
3. Add the cubed butter and cut into the dry ingredients with a pastry cutter or by pulsing several times in the processor. Pulse until coarse crumbs form.
4. Pour buttermilk on top. Fold everything together with a large spoon or silicone spatula until it begins to come together. The dough will be shaggy and crumbly with some wet spots. Pour the dough and any dough crumbles onto a floured work surface, and gently bring together with generously floured hands. Have extra flour nearby and use it often to flour your hands and work surface in this step. Using floured hands or a floured rolling pin, flatten into a ¾ inch thick rectangle. Fold one side into the centre, then the other side. Turn the dough horizontally. Gently flatten into a ¾ inch thick rectangle again. Repeat the folding again. Turn the dough horizontally one more time. Gently flatten into a ¾ inch thick rectangle. Repeat one last time.
5. Cut into 3-inch circles with a biscuit cutter. Re-roll and flatten any scraps and cut more circles until you have about 10-12 biscuits.
6. Arrange on a parchment paper lined baking sheet. Make sure the biscuits are touching.
7. Brush the tops with 2 tbsp heavy cream or buttermilk and sprinkle with coarse sugar. Bake for 18-22 minutes or until the biscuits are golden brown on top. Remove from the oven, then cool in the pan for at least 10 minutes before assembling.
8. Using hand mixer or a stand mixer fitted with a whisk attachment, beat the heavy cream, 2 tbsp granulated sugar, and vanilla on medium-high speed until soft-medium peaks form, ±3 minutes.
9. Slice the biscuits in half and layer with strawberries and whipped cream. Serve immediately.
