# Ingredients
- 1½ lbs small Yukon Gold creamer [[Potato|potatoes]]
- 4 tbsp [[Olive Oil|extra virgin olive oil]]
- 2 oz [[Anchovy|white anchovy fillets]]
- 2 tbsp [[Parsley|parsley]], chopped

# Directions
1. With a rack in hte middle position, preheat the oven to 450°F. Spread the potatoes on a baking sheet and roast until they are very tender, ±35 minutes. Remove the pan from the oven, and set the potatoes aside to cool for  ±10 minutes.
2. When the potatoes are cool enough to handle, transfer to a large bowl. Brush the same baking sheet with half of the olive oil, and sprinkle lightly with [[Salt|salt]] and [[Black Pepper|pepper]].  Return the potatoes to the baking sheet, spaced about 2½ inches apart. With another baking sheet or the bottom of a mug or a potato smasher, sharply press down on the potatoes to break their skins and compress the flesh until about ¾ inch thick. 
3. Brush the potatoes with the remaining olive oil, and sprinkle lightly with salt and pepper. Return the potatoes to the oven and roast until deep golden brown, ±25 minutes, turning the baking sheet halfway through. Taste a small piece of potato and adjust the seasoning with additional salt and pepper, if necessary.
4. Arrange the potatoes on a serving platter, lay an anchovy fillet over each one, and sprinkle with the parsley. Serve immediately.
