# Ingredients
- 1 [[Cucumber|cucumber]]
- 2½ lbs [[Tomatoes|tomatoes]], chopped
- ½ [[Bell Pepper|red bell pepper]], stemmed and seeded
- ¼ [[Red Onion|small red onion]]
- 2 cloves [[Garlic|garlic]]
- ¼ cup [[Cilantro|cilantro]], chopped, plus garnish
- 3 tbsp [[Sherry Vinegar|sherry vinegar]]
- ½ cup [[Olive Oil|extra virgin olive oil]], plus drizzling
- 1¼ tsp [[Salt|sea salt]]
- ¼ tsp [[Black Pepper|pepper]]
- [[Cherry Tomatoes|Cherry tomatoes]] for garnish

# Directions
1. Finely chop ¼ of the cucumber and reserve for garnish.
2. Peel the remaining cucumber, and cut into chunks. Add the chunks, the tomatoes, bell pepper, onion, garlic, cilantro, vinegar, olive oil, salt, and pepper to a blender, and blend until smooth. Season to taste and chill for at least 2 hours.
3. Serve, garnished with the reserved diced cucumber, cherry tomatoes, herbs, drizzles of olive oil, and freshly ground black pepper.
