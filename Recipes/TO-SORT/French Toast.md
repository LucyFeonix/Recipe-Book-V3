# Ingredients
- ⅔ cup [[Milk|milk]]
- 2 [[Egg|eggs]]
- *1 tsp [[Vanilla Extract|vanilla extract]]*
- *¼ tsp ground [[Cinnamon|cinnamon]] or [[Nutmeg|nutmeg]]*
- [[Salt|salt]]
- 6 slices of white [[Bread|bread]]
- 1 tbsp [[Unsalted Butter|unsalted butter]]
# Notes
- 3 servings
- Requirements:
- Time: 
# Directions
1. Whisk the milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt together in a shallow bowl until combined and smooth.
2. Lightly butter a skillet and heat over medium-high heat. Soak each slice of bread in the egg mixture, 5-10 seconds per side, allowing it to absorb the mixture without becoming soggy.
3. Transfer the slice to the hot skillet and cook until golden brown and slightly crisp, 3-4 minutes per side.
4. Serve hot.
