# Ingredients
- 3 [[Chicken Breast|chicken breasts]]
- 1 cup [[Heavy Cream|heavy cream]]
- 1 cup [[Chicken Broth|chicken broth]]
- 1 tbsp [[Corn Starch|corn starch]]
- 1 cup [[Parmesan Cheese|parmesan cheese]], grated
- 3 cloves [[Garlic|garlic]], minced
- 2 tbsp [[Unsalted Butter|unsalted butter]]
- *[[Parsley]] for garnish*
- ±250g [[Pasta|pasta]] (recipe suggests fettucine)
- 2 tbsp [[Paprika|smoked paprika]]
- 2 tbsp [[Garlic Powder|garlic powder]]
- 2 tbsp [[Onion Powder|onion powder]]
- 1 tsp [[Salt|salt]]
- 1 tsp [[Black Pepper|pepper]]
- ½ tsp [[Cayenne Pepper|cayenne pepper]]
# Notes
- 3 servings

- Time: 
# Directions
1.  Combine the smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, pepper, and cayenne pepper, and use this mix to season all sides of the chicken breasts.
2. Cook the chicken in a skillet and flip every 3 minutes until it reaches an internal temperature of 165°F. Remove from the pan, and set aside.
3. Add in the butter and garlic to the skillet and let the garlic cook for 1 minute.
4. Cook the fettuccine noodles until al dente (6-8 minutes)
5. Add in the chicken broth, whipping cream, corn starch, and parmesan cheese. 
6. Add the cooked fettuccine noodles to the pan and thickened sauce, and mix thoroughly.
7. Serve on a plate, and top with sliced chicken and fresh parsley.
