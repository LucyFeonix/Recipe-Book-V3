# Ingredients
## Salisbury Steak
- ½ [[Yellow Onion|onion]]
- ⅓ cup [[Bread Crumbs|breadcrumbs]]
- 500g (1 lb) [[Minced Beef|minced beef]]
- 1 clove [[Garlic|garlic]]
- 1 [[Egg|egg]]
- 2 tbsp [[Ketchup|ketchup]]
- 1 cube [[Beef Stock|beef stock]], crumbled
- ½ tsp [[Worcestershire Sauce|worcestershire sauce]]
- 3 tsp [[Dijon Mustard|dijon mustard]]
## Gravy
- 1 tbsp [[Olive Oil|olive oil]]
- 2 cloves [[Garlic|garlic]], minced
- ½ [[Yellow Onion|onion]], finely chopped
- 150g (5 oz) [[Button Mushrooms|button mushrooms]], sliced
- 30g (2 tbsp) [[Unsalted Butter|unsalted butter]]
- 3 tbsp [[Flour|flour]]
- 2 cups [[Beef Stock|beef stock]]
- ½ cup water
- 2 tsp [[Dijon Mustard|dijon mustard]]
- 1 tsp [[Worcestershire Sauce|worcestershire sauce]]

# Directions
1. Place the breadcrumbs in a bowl. Use a box grater and grate the onion over the breadcrumbs. Mix with your hands, and leave to soak for a few minutes.
2. Add the remaining Salisbury Steak ingredients into the bowl. Use your hands to mix until just combined. Mix well for a couple of minutes, until the mixture becomes somewhat pasty – ensuring that the patties hold together well.
3. Divide into 5 and pat into oval patties around 2-3cm thick.
4. Heat oil in a skillet over high heat. Add the patties, and cook the first side for a minute or until browned. Gently flip, and brown the other side. Remove onto a plate.
5. Add oil to the skillet if necessary. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and cook for 2 minutes, until the onions are a bit translucent.
6. Add the mushrooms into the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes until golden.
7. Turn the heat down to medium, and add the butter. Once melted, add flour and cook for 30 seconds, stirring constantly.
8. Gradually add in beef broth, stirring as you go. Once mostly lump-free, whisk in the remaining gravy ingredients.
9. Add the steaks along with the resting juices on the plate. Cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the gravy is thickened, stirring occasionally around the steaks. If the gravy thickens too quickly, add more water.
10. Adjust the salt and pepper to taste.
11. Serve the steaks topped with the gravy over [[Mashed Potatoes|mashed potatoes]].
