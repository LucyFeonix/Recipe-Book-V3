# Ingredients
- 100g [[Granulated Sugar|granulated sugar]]
- 200g [[Strawberry|strawberries]]
- 100g [[Raspberries|raspberries]]
- 100g [[Cranberry|cranberries]]
- 100g [[Blackberries|blackberries]]
- 50g [[Powdered Sugar|powdered sugar]]
- 20mL [[Limoncello|limoncello]]
- 2 [[Egg|egg whites]]
- [[Champagne|champagne]]

# Directions
1. Heat the sugar with 4 tbsp of water in a pan and let this cook into [[Simple Syrup|simple syrup]]. Take the pan off the fire, and let the syrup cool off.
2. Wash the berries and puree them with the powdered sugar or rub them through a sieve into a bowl.
3. Mix the simple syrup and limoncello into the berry mash. Beat the egg whites until stiff peaks, and mix into the berry mixture.
4. Fill a metal form with the mass, and let this freeze in the freezer while stirring occasionally for 2 hours. 
5. Let the sorbet thaw lightly and put into a piping bag with star-shaped mouthpiece.
6. Pipe some of the sorbet into a glass, and top up with champagne. *Garnish with a cookie.*
