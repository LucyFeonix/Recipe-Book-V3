# Ingredients
- 1 lb [[Minced Meat|minced meat]]
- 1 tsp [[Garlic Powder]]
- 1 tsp [[Onion Powder]]
- 2 tsp [[Worcestershire Sauce]]
- 2 cloves [[Garlic|garlic]]
- Mild [[Cjeddar|cheddar]]
- Shredded [[Mozzarella|mozzarella]]
- 400g [[Pizza Dough|pizza dough]]
- 1 egg, whisked
- [[Parmesan Cheese]] for garnish

# Directions
1. Cook the minced meat on medium heat. Once browned, add minced garlic and all the seasonings. Stir for a minute, and set aside.
2. Preheat the oven to 375F
3. Take the pizza dough and cut it into 5-inch circles. Add two spoonfuls of the seasoned beef, some mozzarella, and a cube of cheddar in the middle.
4. Fold the dough around the filling and pinch the edges tightly to seal. Pull into a tight ball, and rest for 5 minutes.
5. Place the balls seam-side down on a parchment=lined baking sheet. Brush a thin layer of egg wash and sprinkle freshly grated parmesan on top.
6. Bake for 15-18 minutes until deeply golden.
7. Brush on garlic butter and sprinkle more parmesan.
