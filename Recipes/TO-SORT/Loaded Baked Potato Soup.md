# Ingredients
- 6 slices [[Bacon|bacon]]
- 2 tbsp [[Unsalted Butter|unsalted butter]]
- 3 cloves [[Garlic|garlic]], minced
- ½ cup [[Flour|flour]]
- 4 cups [[Chicken Broth|chicken broth]]
- 2 cups [[Milk|milk]]
- 4 [[Russet Potatoes|russet potatoes]], peeled and cut into bite-sized chunks
- ½ tsp [[Salt|salt]]
- ½ tsp [[Black Pepper|pepper]]
- 2 cups [[Cheddar Cheese|cheddar cheese]], shredded
- [[Sour Cream|Sour cream]], for serving
- Additional shredded cheddar for serving
- Sliced [[Scallions|scallions]] for serving
# Notes
- 4 servings

- Time: 
# Directions
1. Start by adding 6 slices of bacon to a large soup pot. Cook until the bacon is very crispy. Turn off the heat, and transfer the bacon to a paper towel lined plate.
2. Let the bacon grease cool for about 5 minutes, then add the butter. Once it is melted, add the garlic and the flour. Turn the heat back on and let this cook for bout a minute.
3. Add 1 cup of chicken broth and stir it until it’s smooth. Repeat with a second cup of chicken broth. At this point, start trying to scrape up any of the brown bits at the bottom of the pot. Add the remaining chicken broth.
4. Add 2 cups of milk, the potatoes, and the salt and pepper. Stir it up, and let come to a simmer. Let it cook for about 15 minutes.
5. Stir in the shredded cheddar, and let it simmer for another 5 minutes as the potatoes cook.
6. Chop up the bacon.
7. Serve with sour cream, shredded cheddar, sliced scallions, and bacon bits.
