# Ingredients
- 1 lb [[Chicken Breast|chicken breast]]
- 3 tbsp [[Parmesan Cheese|parmesan cheese]], grated
- 1 tsp [[Italian Seasoning|Italian seasoning]]
- ½ tsp [[Garlic Powder|garlic powder]]
- ½ tsp [[Onion Powder|onion powder]]
- [[Salt]] & [[Black Pepper|pepper]]
- 3 tbsp [[Olive Oil|olive oil]]
- ¼ cup [[Mayonnaise|mayonnaise]]
- 2 tbsp [[Lemon|lemon juice]]
- 1 tbsp [[White Miso Paste|white miso paste]]
- 1 tsp [[Worcestershire Sauce|worcestershire sauce]]
- 1 tsp [[Anchovy Paste|anchovy paste]]
- 1 clove [[Garlic|garlic]], minced
- ⅓ cup [[Neutral Oil|neutral oil]]
- 4 large [[Tortilla|tortillas]]
- 2 heads [[Romaine Lettuce|romaine lettuce]]
- 1 cup [[Croutons|croutons]]
- ¼ cup [[Parmesan Cheese]], grated
# Notes
- 4 servings

- Time: 
# Directions
1. Place the chicken on a cutting board and carefully slice each breast in half horizontally to form two thin cutlets.
2. To a small bowl, add a little less than half of the parmesan, the italian seasoning, the garlic and onion powders, as well as a pinch of salt and pepper, and toss to combine. Spread the seasoning mixture evenly over both sides of the chicken, and press onto the breast to adhere.
3. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Arrange the chicken in the pan, and cook for 7-10 minutes, carefully flipping once halfway through. The chicken should measure in at 165°F. Transfer to a clean cutting board and let cool while you prepare the remainder of the recipe.
4. Prepare the miso-caesar dressing: combine the mayonnaise, lemon juice, miso paste, worcestershire sauce, anchovy paste, and garlic into a medium bowl. Gradually pour in the oil while whisking constantly until the dressing is smooth and creamy. Bring to taste with black pepper if needed.
5. Place the romaine lettuce in a large mixing bowl. Cut the chicken into small, bite-sized pieces and add to the bowl, along with the croutons and parmesan. Add the dressing, and toss to combine.
6. Warm the tortillas until pliable. Lay it on the work surface and add ~2 cups of salad mixture, leaving a 2-inch border all the way around. Wrap, and enjoy.
