 # Ingredients
- 6 tbsp [[Unsalted Butter|unsalted butter]]
- 2 tbsp [[Flour|all-purpose flour]]
- 1 tbsp [[Paprika|paprika]]
- ½ tsp [[Garlic Powder|garlic powder]]
- 1 pinch [[Cayenne Pepper|cayenne pepper]]
- 3 cups [[Milk|milk]]
- 2 tsp [[Dijon Mustard|dijon mustard]]
- 2 oz [[Cream Cheese|cream cheese]], cut into 4 pieces
- 4 ½ cups [[Cheddar Cheese|cheddar cheese]]
- 12 oz [[Fusilli|fusilli]]
- 1 ½ cup [[Gruyère Cheese|gruyère cheese]]
- 1 ½ cups [[Bread Crumbs|breadcrumbs]]

# Directions
1. With a rack in the middle position, preheat the oven to 375 °F. Smear a shallow 13 by 9-inch baking dish with 2 tablespoons of the butter. Set on a baking sheet, and set aside.
2. Fill a large pot with water, set over high heat, and bring to a boil.
3. Meanwhile, in another large pot over medium-high heat, melt 2 tablespoons of butter. Whisk in the flour, paprika, garlic powder, and cayenne, and cook, scraping the bottom of the pot, for 1 minute. 
4. Whisking constantly, gradually add the milk. Bring to a strong simmer, whisking the liquid and scraping the bottom of the pan often. Adjust the heat to medium and continue simmering and whisking until the sauce is thickened to the consistency of half-and-half, about 8 minutes longer.
5. Remove the pot from the heat; add the mustard, cream cheese, 1 tsp [[Salt|salt]], 1 tsp [[Black Pepper|pepper]], and whisk vigorously to melt and incorporate the cream cheese. Whisking constantly but gently, add the cheddar, about a cup at a time, taking care to fully melt and incoorporate each addition before adding the next. The sauce should be smooth and uniform.
6. Add 1 tbsp salt and the fusilli to the pot of boiling water and cook, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking, according to the package directions until 2 minutes shy of al dente. Drain the pasta, add it to the sauce, and stir to coat thoroughly. Let the mixture cool briefly, add the gruyère, and stir to incorporate. Adjust the seasoning with additional salt and pepper if necessary. Scrape the entire mixture into the prepared baking dish.
7. Melt the remaining butter. Add the bread crumbs and ¼ tsp salt, season with pepper, and toss to coat the breadcrumbs. Sprinkle evenly over the pasta.
8. Bake the pasta until heated through, the surface is browned, and the edges are bubbly, 30-40 minutes. Rotate the pan halfway through. Let rest 8-10 minutes before serving.
