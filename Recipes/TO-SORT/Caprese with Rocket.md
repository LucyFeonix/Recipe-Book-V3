# Ingredients
- 8 [[Tomato|tomatoes]]
- 2 balls [[Mozzarella|mozzarella]]
- 100g [[Rocket|rocket]]
- 50g [[Cress|cress]]
- 100g [[Parma Ham|parma ham]]
- 7.5mL [[Balsamic Vinegar|balsamic vinegar]]
- 5mL [[Olive Oil|olive oil]]

# Directions
1. Wash and core the tomatoes, and drain the mozzarella. Cut the tomatoes and mozzarella into thin slices.
2. Wash the rocket. Arrange rocket, cut cress, tomato slices, mozzarella slices, and ham on a serving platter.
3. Flavour the caprese with salt and pepper. Drip the balsamic vinegar and olive oil over it, and let sit for about 5 minutes. Serve with [[Ciabatta|ciabatta]].
