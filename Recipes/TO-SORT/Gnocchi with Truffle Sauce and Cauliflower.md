# Ingredients
- 15g [[Unsalted Butter|unsalted butter]]
- 300g [[Yellow Onion|yellow onion]], diced
- 2 cloves [[Garlic|garlic]]
- 2 tbsp [[Flour|flour]]
- 80g [[Truffle Tapenade|truffle tapenade]]
- 250ml [[Heavy Cream|heavy cream]]
- 90g [[Parmigiano Reggiano|parmesan cheese]], finely grated
- 400g [[Cauliflower|cauliflower]] florets
- 1kg [[Gnocchi|gnocchi]]
- 7.5g [[Sweet Basil|basil]]
# Notes
- 3 servings
- Requirements:
- Time: 
# Directions
1. Bring a large pan of water to a boil.
2. Heat the butter in a skillet and fry the onion for 5-6 minutes with [[Salt|salt]] and [[Black Pepper|pepper]] on medium high heat. In the meantime, dice the garlic, add, and fry with for a minute. Mix in the flour, and brown for two minutes.
3. Add the tapenade, heavy cream, 150ml of boiling water, and half of the cheese, and bring to a boil on high heat. Sudder for 5 minutes. Flavour with salt and pepper.
4. Cut the florets into 3cm pieces, and cook together with the gnocchi for 4 minutes. Roughly chop the basil.
5. Mix the cauliflower and gnocchi through the sauce. Garnish with the remaining cheese and the basil, and serve.
