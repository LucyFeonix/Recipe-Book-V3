# Ingredients
## Filling
- 3 lbs [[Peach|peaches]], firm but ripe, peeled, pitted, and cut into 1½-inch wedges
- 3 tbsp [[Granulated Sugar|granulated sugar]]
- 1 pinch [[Salt|kosher salt]]
- 3 tbsp [[Brown Sugar|brown sugar]]
- 1 tbsp [[Corn Starch|cornstarch]]
- ¼ tsp [[Cloves|cloves]], ground
- 3 tbsp [[Bourbon|bourbon]], amber or dark [[Rum|rum]], [[Brandy|brandy]] or [[Cognac|cognac]]
- 1 tsp [[Vanilla Extract|vanilla extract]]
- 1 cup [[Blueberries|blueberries]]
## Topping
-  5 tbsp [[Granulated Sugar|granulated sugar]]
- ¼ tsp [[Ground Cinnamon|ground cinnamon]]
- 1½ cups [[Flour|all-purpose flour]]
- 1½ tsp [[Baking Powder|baking powder]]
- ½ tsp [[Salt|kosher salt]]
- 1 [[Egg|egg]]
- ⅓ cup [[Buttermilk|buttermilk]]
- 3 tbsp [[Unsalted Butter|unsalted butter]], melted and cooled
- 1 tsp [[Vanilla Extract|vanilla extract]]

# Directions
## Filling
1. In a large strainer set over a large bowl, toss the peaches, granulated sugar, and salt, and set aside to drain for 1 hour, tossing them once or twice during that time. Pour off the accumulated peach juices and reserve for another use, if desired.
2. In a small bowl, combine the brown sugar, cornstarch, cloves, bourbon and vanilla and whisk to incorporate.
3. Add the blueberries to the peaches, then add the cornstarch mixture and toss to coat. Scrape the filling into an 8-inch square pan with 2-inch sides, and spread into an even layer. Set aside.
