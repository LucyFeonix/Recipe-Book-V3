# Ingredients
- 1 1/2 cups pitted [[Olive|olives]]
- 4 oil-packed [[Sun-dried Tomatoes|sun-dried tomatoes]]
- 3 tbsp [[Olive Oil|extra virgin olive oil]]
- 1 tbsp [[Capers|capers]]
- 1/2 tbsp [[Lemon|lemon juice]]
- 1 clove [[Garlic|garlic]]
- 1/3 cup [[Sweet Basil|basil]] or [[Parsley|parsley]]
# Notes
- 4 servings
- Requirements: mortar & pestle / food processor
- Time: 10 minutes
# Directions
## Food processor
1.  Combine the ingredients in a small food processor.
2. Pulse until combined, but still chunky. Add the fresh herbs, and pulse again. The mixture should be smooth, but still a little bit chunky.
