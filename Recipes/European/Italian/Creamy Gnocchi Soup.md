# Ingredients
- 700g [[Sugocasa Erbe|sugocasa erbe]]
- 500g [[Gnocchi|gnocchi]]
- 300g [[Italian Sausage|italian sausage]]
- 1 [[Leek|leek]], cut into half-rings
- 1 [[Shallots|shallot]], diced
- 3 cloves [[Garlic|garlic]], minced
- 1L [[Vegetable stock|vegetable stock]] or [[Chicken Stock|chicken stock]]
- 240mL [[Heavy Cream|heavy cream]]
- 2 handfuls [[Spinach|spinach]]
- *[[Parmesan Cheese|Parmesan cheese]] to taste*
- [[Chilli Flakes|Chilli flakes]] to taste
- [[Olive Oil|Olive oil]]
- [[Salt|Salt]] and [[Black Pepper|pepper]] to taste

# Directions
1. Heat some olive oil in a large, deep pan. Fry the leek and garlic for 2-3 minutes.
2. Add the Italian sausage, and fry until lightly browned.
3. Add the sugocasa and stock. Bring to a boil, and simmer for 5-10 minutes.
4. Add the uncooked gnocchi and boil until soft and done, (±3 minutes).
5. Add the heavy cream and spinach, and stir. Let the spinach wilt.
6. Add salt, pepper, and chilli flakes to taste, and serve, *garnished with parmesan.*
