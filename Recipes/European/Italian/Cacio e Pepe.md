# Ingredients
- 350g [[Pasta|spaghetti or linguine]]
- 175g [[Cheese|Pecorino Romano]]
- 2 tsp [[Black Pepper|black peppercorns]]
- [[Olive Oil|olive oil]]
# Notes
- 4 servings
- Requirements:  pot, pan, mortar & pestle
- Time: 20 min
# Directions
1. Cook the pasta in salted water. Fry the peppercorns on low heat in a dry pan until fragrant (+-5 min)
2. Take the pan off the fire, and crush the peppercorns in a mortar and pestle until roughly ground.
3. In a separate, cold pan, mix a teaspoon of olive oil with a dash of the pasta's cooking liquid, and mix with a handful of the grated cheese.\
4. Drain the pasta, keeping the cooking liquid. Mix the pasta through the cheese-oil mixture, and put the pan on low heat. Add the remaining cheese, together with the black pepper and cooking liquid to taste.
