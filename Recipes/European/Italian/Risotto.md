# Ingredients
- 1.2L [[Chicken Stock|chicken stock]]
- 1 [[Yellow Onion|yellow onion]], diced
- 60g [[unsalted butter|unsalted butter]]
- 300g [[Arborio Rice|arborio rice]]
- 100ml [[White Wine|dry white wine]]
- 75g [[Parmigiano Reggiano|parmigiano reggiano]]
# Notes
- 4 servings
- Requirements:
- Time: 
# Directions
1. Bring the stock to a boil on high heat, then turn the heat to low. Heat half of the butter in a thick pan. Add the onion, and fry lightly until glassy.
2. Add the rice, and stir-fry for three minutes until glassy. Add the white wine, and stir until this has been absorbed.
3. Gradually add spoonfuls of the chicken stock, and gently stir until absorbed. Repeat until the rice is ready, ±20 minutes.
4. While adding the stock, grate the parmesan cheese. Take the pan off the heat, and add the cheese and the remaining butter, and let sit for 3 minutes with a lid on. Take the lid off, stir, and add [[Salt|salt]] and [[Black Pepper|pepper]] to taste. Garnish with additional parmesan and pepper, and serve.
